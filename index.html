THE STORY

My Grandmother LOVES these silly songs that I wrote for my kids

She had been telling me for some time that I needed to record them.

I asked her for help paying for a coding school.

She very calmly asked me...

"Have you recorded those songs yet?"

The Great American Rat Race was getting in the way.

I had not yet recorded those songs.

Coding school was starting soon.

I knew what I must do.

My Grandmother LOVES Jimmy Kimmel.

Maybe his biggest fan. We struck a deal.

She thought that Jimmy would love my songs too.

She would help me, if I recorded my songs.

And "send them in" to the Jimmy Kimmel show.

THE PLAYERS

My Grandmother

Patsy Rampy is a self made lady who rules the high plains with a delightful smile. She likes fine wine and playing bridge. Her fortunes were made in real estate. She knows how to fix a mean special breakfast. She likes Las Vegas and late night talk shows. My Grandmother loves Jimmy Kimmel.

Jimmy Kimmel

Jimmy Kimmel is a self made man who rules the late night airwaves with delightful jokes. He likes mean tweets and re-runs of the Man Show. His fortunes were made in comedy. He knows how to turn an immigrant into a star. He likes Brooklyn and Austin Texas. Everyone loves Jimmy Kimmel.

Me

Matthew Rampy is a regular American doofus who has no idea what he is doing. He likes vodka and playing golf. He is still searching for his path to fame and fortune. He sometimes writes humorous songs for his kids. He likes Lake Tahoe and making art. He has no opinion on Jimmy Kimmel.

Isabel and Griffin

Isabel and Griffin are the apples of thier Dada's eye. Isabel likes the Princess Industrial Complex. Griffin likes destroying things. The only thing they know about Jimmy Kimmel is that he was larger than life on marketing wraps on the commuter trains behind our house during the SXSW shows.
